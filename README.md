# Dental-Case
I Started by Measuring my toothbrush, Mirror, and Dental Wax Case.

## Design Process

<img width="1166" height="442" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fcdbaf02-6d1a-48fd-96ca-b3ebae7fa4a4" />

### Step 1: Toothbrush Layout
After which I made a blocky sketch of the toothbrush. I rotated it side ways so i dont have to print a massive case.

### Step 2: Mirror Slot
Then I made a circle the size of the mirror and added a semi circle so you can grab it out of the case.

### Step 3: Wax Holder
Next I made a simle rectangle for the wax.

### Step 4: Case Base
Once all the parts were layout i gave them an offset so they would actually fit in the case and I exturded the case.

<img width="524" height="481" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ace284bc-107b-47ba-ba66-0f0239ddcb51" />

### Step 5: Honeycomb Pattern
Then I sketch a honeycomb pattern and extuded it onto the case.

<img width="529" height="439" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aae4908b-9a86-46cb-80b8-408b7f456693" />

### Step 6: Lid Design
After this I created a sketch ontop of the case and traced the same box and i added a 3-4mm lip to open the case.

<img width="574" height="235" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcd935db-747a-4f99-936d-043820795d0f" />

### Step 7: Hinge Mechanism
I created a joint/hinge and attched it to the case top and bottom. 

To make it i made a circle with a triangle connected to it on left and right side, 

<img width="321" height="222" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff991ad1-9469-49bf-a850-1795ac6af16c" />

Then i extruded one side, left triangle + circle, and the same for the right and added a cone to one of the edges and cut that into the other triangle.

<img width="246" height="197" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/24bc0907-a00d-40a6-a1de-410570127e32" />

### Step 8: Final Assembly
After this I mirror it together and connected it all to the cases.
